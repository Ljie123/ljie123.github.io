About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Software System Security Assurance Group at Tsinghua University. I received BS degree in computer science from the Beijing University of Post and Telecommunication, China, in 2017.6. I got my Ph.D. degree in software engineering under the supervision of Prof. Jiaguang Sun from Tsinghua University in 2022.6.

My research interests include vulnerability detection, fuzzing, and software engineering.
